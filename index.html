2D Shapes

Triangle
Perimeter P Area A Right triangle hypotenuse c Semi‑perimeter s
  • Perimeter: P=a+b+c
  • Area (with base b, height h): A=12bh
  • Heron's formula:
    Let s=a+b+c2. Then
    A=s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)
  • Pythagorean theorem (right triangle): c2=a2+b2
  • Law of cosines: c2 = a2 + b2 - 2ab⁢cos⁡γ
Square
side adiagonal d
  • Perimeter: P=4a
  • Area: A=a2
  • Diagonal: d=a2
Rectangle
length lwidth wdiagonal d
  • Perimeter: P=2(l+w)
  • Area: A=lw
  • Diagonal: d=l2+w2
Parallelogram
adjacent sides a,bheight h
  • Perimeter: P=2(a+b)
  • Area: A=bh
Rhombus
diagonals d1, d2
  • Perimeter: P=4a (all sides equal to a)
  • Area: A=d1⋅d22
Trapezoid (US) / Trapezium (UK)
bases b1, b2height h
  • Perimeter: Sum of all side lengths.
  • Area: A= b1+b2 2 h
  • Median length: m=b1+b22
Circle
radius rdiameter d=2r
  • Circumference: C=2πr=πd
  • Area: A=πr2
  • Sector (angle θ in radians):
    Arc length L=θr, Area A=θ2r2
Ellipse
semi‑major asemi‑minor b
  • Area: A=πab
  • Circumference (Ramanujan approx.): C≈ π [ 3(a+b) - (3a+b) (a+3b) ]
Regular n-gon
side length sapothem an sides
  • Perimeter: P=ns
  • Area (two forms): A=Pa2=ns24tan(πn)

3D Shapes

Cube
edge a
  • Surface area: SA=6a2
  • Volume: V=a3
  • Space diagonal: d=a3
Rectangular Prism (Cuboid)
length lwidth wheight h
  • Surface area: SA=2(lw+lh+wh)
  • Volume: V=lwh
  • Space diagonal: d=l2+w2+h2
Right Prism (General base)
base area Abasebase perimeter Pbaseheight h
  • Lateral area: LA=Pbaseh
  • Surface area: SA=LA+2Abase
  • Volume: V=Abaseh
Right Circular Cylinder
radius rheight h
  • Lateral area: LA=2πrh
  • Surface area: SA=2πrh+2πr2
  • Volume: V=πr2h
Right Circular Cone
radius rheight hslant s
  • Slant height: s=r2+h2
  • Surface area: SA=πrs+πr2
  • Volume: V=13πr2h
Right Square Pyramid
base side bheight hslant (face) s
  • Slant height (face center): s=b22+h2
  • Surface area: SA=b2+2bs
  • Volume: V=13b2h
Sphere
radius r
  • Surface area: SA=4πr2
  • Volume: V=43πr3
Hemisphere
radius r
  • Curved surface area (no base): CSA=2πr2
  • Total surface area (with base): SA=3πr2
  • Volume: V=23πr3
Frustum of a Right Circular Cone
top radius r₁bottom radius r₂height hslant s
  • Slant height: s=h2+(r_2-r_1)2
  • Lateral area: LA=π(r1+r2)s
  • Surface area: SA=LA+πr12+πr22
  • Volume: V=13πh(r12+r1r2+r22)
Frustum of a Right Regular Pyramid
top base area A₁, perimeter P₁bottom base area A₂, perimeter P₂height hslant s
  • Lateral area (regular): LA=12(P1+P2)s
  • Surface area: SA=LA+A1+A2
  • Volume (general): V=13h(A1+A2+A1A2)
