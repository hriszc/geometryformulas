Double Angle Formulas

  • sin(2x) = 2sin(x)cos(x)
  • cos(2x) = cos(x)2 - sin(x)2 = 2cos(x)2 -1 = 1-2sin(x)2
  • tan(2x) = 2tan(x) 1-tan(x)2
